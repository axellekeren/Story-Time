Fairy Tales

The Enchanted Forest

Lily, a kind-hearted girl from a village near an enchanted forest, frees a fairy named Sparkle. Sparkle tasks Lily with protecting the forest by finding the Tree of Wisdom. Throughout her journey, Lily spreads kindness to all creatures she meets. In the end, Lily's kindness ensures the forest's safety and prosperity.

The Princess and the Pea

A son wants to marry a real princess. One night, in a terrible storm, a princess came to their castle. To make sure that she was a real princess, the old queen placed a pea under twenty mattresses and twenty goose feather beds. The next morning, the daughter admitted that she could not sleep because she felt something hard under her mattress. Thus, they knew that she was a real princess, and the son married her.

Little Red Riding Hood

A little girl who always wore a red robe went to visit her grandmother who lived across the forest, bringing cake and butter. In the forest, he met a cunning wolf. The wolf disguises itself as her grandmother to trick Little Red Riding Hood, but in the end they are rescued by a hunter who passes through the woods and chases the wolf. Little Red Riding Hood and her grandmother survived the danger.

The Frog Prince

A princess lost her favorite golden ball in the palace pool. A frog offered to return it if the princess promised to be his friend and let him live in the palace. Putri agrees, but forgets her promise after getting the ball back. That night, the frog came to the palace to remind the princess of her promise. Reluctantly, the princess fulfilled her promise. When she finally kisses the frog, the frog turns into a handsome prince who has been cursed by a witch. They fall in love and live happily ever after.

Princess Aurora and the Golden Bird

Princess Aurora from a peaceful kingdom sets out to find a golden bird that can bring eternal happiness. On his journey, he met an old woman who gave him a magic compass. The compass helps him navigate the obstacle-filled forest, avoiding an evil witch who also wants the bird. After facing many challenges, Aurora managed to save the golden bird and exorcise the evil witch. When the golden bird arrived in the kingdom, it spread the light of happiness throughout the land. Aurora was praised for her bravery, and the kingdom lived in eternal happiness and peace.

The Adventures of Nia and the Magic Crystal

Nia, a young girl from a small village, finds a Magic Crystal that has the power to grant one wish for good. When her village was attacked by an evil giant, Nia sincerely prayed that the village would be protected. The Magic Crystal summons the guardian spirits of the forest who then drive away the giant and promise to protect the village forever. The village returns to peace, and Nia is hailed as a hero for her bravery. His story has been told from generation to generation, showing that courage and kindness always bring good results.

The Kingdom of the Stars and Three Jewels

The Star Empire faces a curse that will extinguish the stars in the sky. Princess Lyra sets out on a quest to find three magical gems—the Gem of Wisdom, the Gem of Courage, and the Gem of Kindness—that can undo the curse. On her journey, Lyra faces various obstacles and uses her intelligence, courage and kindness to obtain the three gems. After returning to her kingdom, Lyra uses the gems to restore the light of the stars in the sky. The Star Kingdom is once again bright and lives in peace, with Princess Lyra revered as a hero.

Princess Elara and the Tree of Wishes

Princess Elara lives in a peaceful kingdom with her father, a king who has fallen seriously ill. Hearing about the Wish Tree that can grant one wish, Elara decides to look for it in order to cure her father. On his journey, he overcomes various obstacles and gets help from magical creatures. Finally, Elara finds the Tree of Wishes and begs for her father to be healed. His request was granted, and his father recovered. Elara returned to the kingdom with joy and was greeted with joy by the people and her father who was healed.
